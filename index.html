Born deep out west, The Engineer was made for Mechanical duties. Without his trusty wrench and tool gun, he's without purpose.

The Duties of The Engineer were soully to repair PLanes and War Machines during The Great War. Though things took a turn when his village came under attack.

Learning the ways of Defense and even some Potion-works, The Engineer was able to combine his skill with his Engineering Knowledge, and crafted his way into the support class he is known for.
